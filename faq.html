Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Find everything you need to know about our services below.

Nyvex is a premium mobile auto detailing service. We come to your location — home, office, or anywhere convenient — and provide professional car care including ceramic coating, paint correction, interior detailing, and foam wash.

We currently serve the entire city and surrounding areas within a 30km radius. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, just enter your address during booking and we'll confirm availability.

Not necessarily. As long as we have access to the vehicle and a water/electricity source (if needed), you can go about your day. We'll notify you once the service is complete.

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to your car's exterior that creates a protective layer. It repels water, resists UV damage, and keeps your car looking glossy. Depending on the grade, it can last anywhere from 1 to 5 years with proper maintenance.

Our interior detailing includes deep vacuuming, dashboard and console cleaning, leather/fabric conditioning, door panel cleaning, air vent dusting, glass cleaning from inside, and odor elimination. We treat every surface to leave your cabin fresh and spotless.

Paint correction is the process of removing surface imperfections like swirl marks, light scratches, oxidation, and water spots using machine polishing. It restores your paint to a mirror-like finish and is often done before applying ceramic coating.

Yes! Engine bay cleaning is available as an add-on service. We carefully degrease and clean the engine compartment, dress rubber and plastic components, and leave it looking showroom-fresh.

You can book through our website, call us directly, or message us on WhatsApp. Simply choose your service, pick a date and time, provide your location, and we'll confirm your appointment.

Yes. You can reschedule free of charge up to 12 hours before your appointment. Cancellations made 24+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Please refer to our Terms & Conditions for the full cancellation policy.

Service duration varies: Foam Wash takes about 1-2 hours, Interior Detailing 2-3 hours, Paint Correction 4-6 hours, and Ceramic Coating 6-8 hours (including curing time). We'll give you an estimated time during booking.

Pricing depends on the service and vehicle size. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You can view our price list on the Services page or get an instant quote during booking. Packages start from ₹999 for a basic foam wash.

Yes, pricing is based on vehicle size. Sedans, hatchbacks, SUVs, and luxury vehicles each have different rates due to the surface area and time required. The exact price is shown during booking based on your vehicle type.

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash. Payment is collected after service completion unless you opt for prepaid booking.

Avoid washing your car for 7 days after application. After that, use pH-neutral car shampoo, avoid automatic car washes, and do a maintenance wash every 2 weeks. We also offer maintenance packages to keep your coating in top condition.

Yes! Our ceramic coating comes with a warranty ranging from 1-5 years depending on the package. Paint correction and detailing services include a 7-day satisfaction guarantee — if you're not happy, we'll redo it free of charge.

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with the results, contact us within 7 days and we'll schedule a free redo. We stand behind our work 100%.
