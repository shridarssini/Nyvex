Our Story

Nyvex was born from a simple belief — every car deserves premium care, no matter where you are. What started as a one-man mobile detailing service has grown into a trusted team of skilled technicians delivering excellence at your doorstep.

We saw how inconvenient traditional car washes were — long queues, rushed jobs, and inconsistent results. So we built something different. A service that comes to you, uses only the best products, and treats every vehicle like it's our own.

Today, Nyvex serves hundreds of happy customers with ceramic coatings, paint corrections, interior detailing, and more — all without you leaving your home or office.
